Open-source hardware and software for a citywide LoRa sensor network

Technology deep dive

We have open-sourced the hardware design for our "Tulip bulbs", making it easy to contribute to the ever growing TULIP network. We utilize the "Things Network" - a completely free and open-source telecommunications network designed specifically for the Internet of Things, powered by the LoRa protocol.

Benefits for the community

Cool cities and clean air result in positive social, economic and broader environmental outcomes, with complex interconnecting processes and feedbacks in play. We know these relationships exist, however there is little data available to support specific understanding of these relationships.
